Guidelines For a better donation

We Accept only gently used or new books. Books should be clean, without excessive markings, torn pages, or water damage. We Discard books that are heavily damaged or in poor condition, as they might not be usable by recipients.

Prioritize books that are relevant to the target audience. Consider the age-appropriateness of the books. You can viwe books Separatly by categories suitable for children, teenagers, and adults.

Focus on donating books that have educational value, such as textbooks, reference materials, educational workbooks, and non-fiction books.

If you are donating in bulk, Include a variety of fiction and literature books to encourage reading for pleasure and to nurture imagination and creativity.

Ensure that the books you donated do not promote harmful stereotypes or offend any particular cultural or religious group.

Include recent publications when possible, as they often contain the most up-to-date information.

If the book donation operates towords a specific region or country, prioritize books written in the local language to increase accessibility for the community.

Encourage diverse voices and perspectives by including books from various authors, cultures, and backgrounds.
